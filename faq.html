FAQ's

Who is Team Todo?

Head over to our 'Meet the Team' page to find out who we are and what we do.

Where are you based?

Todo HQ resides in bustling Karachi. It's said to be one of the happiest cities in Pakistan and we couldn't agree more!

How do you take your photos?

Our Creative Producer uses a Canon 5D Mark III, with a mixture of lenses including the Canon 24-70mm. We also use a Manfrotto 3-section tripod for flatylays and stop-motions. We occasionally use the iPhone 8 Plus for other images!

Do you collaborate with brands?

We do! If you’re a brand interested in collaborating, please contact Rameen at tauseeframeen@gmail.com.

How do I contribute to Todo?

Although most of our content is born in-house, we’re always looking for contributors to write about what they're most passionate about. Email us at rameen@.co.pk for more information.

Do you have a PO box?

We do! Email us at enquiries@rameen.co.pk for more information.

Any advice on growing your blog?

If you are starting out with a blog of your own and are eager to see growth, I would say engagement, content, and consistency are all key. Engage with readers on all social media platforms, stick to content you believe in, and try and stay as consistent as possible (that’s the hard part!)

What motivates you to write your blog?

I have an immense passion for writing,. (i also enjoy editing the words of my fellow writers.) styling outfits and taking photos are fun, but my favorite part of the blogging experience is creating written content for Todo. instagram and other social media platforms are great to peruse, but i most enjoy sitting at my computer (cup of coffee in hand) with a “new post” window open. while you may not have asked this, my least favorite part? putting away equiments post photo shoot.

How long does it take you to write a blog post?

a blog post can take anywhere from an hour to several hours to write. however, that does not include the time spent styling the outfit(s), taking photos, editing photos, and cross-promoting the post on social media. all in all, blogging is a fun, yet time-consuming hobby!

Are you hiring interns or assistants?

Although we could definitely use one, unfortunately at this time we are not looking for an intern or an assistant.
